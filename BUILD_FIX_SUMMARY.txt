## 📊 Technical Details

**What is Three.js?**
- JavaScript 3D graphics library
- Used for WebGL rendering
- Enables 3D animations and effects
- Required by FloatingLines component

**Where is it used?**
- `frontend/src/components/FloatingLines.jsx` imports and uses:
  - Scene, Camera, Renderer for 3D
  - Mesh, Geometry for shapes
  - ShaderMaterial for visual effects
  - Vector2, Vector3 for math
